How to Play?

1-there is two modes either you can play with your friend or against the Computure
2-in two player mode each player will have a turn the turn end when the player either press hold or when push the current and the number appears is One
3-in the mode against computure player one plays at start then the computure plays its turn when player one end his turn
4-when pressing on push current button a number between 1 and 6 is generated if the number is 1 active player loses his current score and switch to the other player occurs if the number is anything except 1 the number is stored in the active player current score
5-when pressing on hold all the current score of the active player is added to the total score of this player
6-the first player reach 60 point win the game
